Eight cities, one launch playbook

The Brief

Open new F&B outlets across India without losing brand consistency or momentum.

The Move

Built a repeatable launch framework — media coverage, influencer collabs, on-ground activations — and ran it from a standing start of zero outlets.

The Result

Scaled to 8 outlets (plus a 9th deal closed before transition) across Jalandhar, Agra, Greater Noida, Chandigarh, Noida, Jaipur and Gurugram — each launch drawing 500–1,000 attendees.

Turning delivery apps into growth channels

The Brief

Outlets needed real visibility on Swiggy and Zomato — being listed wasn't enough.

The Move

Went straight to HQ and negotiated central partnerships covering every outlet at once, instead of one-off, outlet-by-outlet deals.

The Result

Brought commission down to 23% (from the standard 30%) across every outlet, and drove a ~30% increase in order discoverability.

Colour Book Strategy & Waah Biryani Week
